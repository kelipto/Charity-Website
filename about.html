About Us

We are a team of enthusiastic individuals who believe in the power of public expression to hold businesses and politicians accountable for their actions. Our web app allows users to create and sign causes related to ethical issues such as creative tax avoidance, unfair employment practices, racism, disregard of environmental impact and other unethical business and political practices.

We believe that everyone has the power to make a difference and by coming together, we can create a movement that promotes ethical behavior and responsible actions. Our platform aims to provide a space for like-minded individuals to connect and support each other in their efforts to make the world a better place.

As a team, we are committed to providing a user-friendly and visually appealing platform that is accessible to all. We understand the importance of transparency and accountability, and we are dedicated to ensuring that our platform operates in a fair and ethical manner.
